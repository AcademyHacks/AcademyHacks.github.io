What is AcademyHacks?

AcademyHacks is a high school hackathon geared towards helping students learn how to code. We are one of the first high school hackathons and guarantee the event will be awesome.

What is a hackathon?

A hackathon is a 24 hour event where teams of students come together to build something awesome and demo it to a panel of judges.

Who can attend? Are there teams?

Any high school student can attend! You can work in teams of 2-4 people, and if you don't have a team, it's ok, we will help you find one.

How much does AcademyHacks cost and how do we get in?

AcademyHacks is completely free! You will need money to get to the event, we will take care of the rest. To get in, just register! If the event fills up we will notify you, so register while you can! We gurantee you will have an awesome time.

What if I can't code?

That is perfectly fine! One of the main purposes of a hackathon is to allow students to learn how to code or build off of what they already know. AcademyHacks aims to help students learn how to program and explore new platforms to create an amazing application.

What should I bring?

Bring yourself and your imagination! Besides that, you should have a laptop and charger, school ID, phone and charger, power strips, and any other electronics or essentials you may need during the event. The event is completely free so we will provide food, but you can also bring your own.

Can I be a sponsor?

Yes, of course! Just click the "Sponsor" button and fill out the form and we will be in touch.

Can I be a mentor?

Yup! Just click the "Mentor" button and register!

What if I have more questions?

If you have any other questions, feel free to contact us at academyhackathon@gmail.com!
